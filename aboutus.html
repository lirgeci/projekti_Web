About us

At Istudio, we transform spaces into inspiring, functional, and aesthetically pleasing environments. Our team of creative designers combines innovation, elegance, and practicality to bring your vision to life. From residential projects to commercial interiors, we focus on personalized solutions that reflect your style and enhance your lifestyle. Every detail matters, and we are committed to delivering spaces that are beautiful, comfortable, and timeless. At Istudio, design is not just about decoration—it's about creating experiences that resonate with those who live, work, and interact within the space. Let us help you reimagine your surroundings and turn your interior dreams into reality.



Every project is a collaboration. We listen to your ideas, understand your needs, and translate them into spaces that are both functional and inspiring. Our design philosophy blends modern aesthetics with timeless elegance, ensuring that each interior not only looks stunning but also feels comfortable and livable. From initial concept to final execution, we are committed to high-quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and exceptional service. Whether it’s a cozy home, a stylish office, or a commercial space, Istudio is your partner in creating interiors that reflect your unique personality and lifestyle. We don’t just design spaces—we create environments that people love to live, work, and interact in.



At Istudio, our mission is to turn your vision into beautiful, functional spaces. We strive to combine creativity, innovation, and quality to make every interior truly unique and welcoming.







iStudio began as a simple idea born from a passion for creating spaces that are both beautiful and functional. The founders, inspired by the power of design to transform everyday life, started working on small residential projects, learning and growing with each client interaction. Every home and office taught them something new, shaping their approach and vision.

As the team gained experience, Istudio expanded into larger and more diverse projects, including modern offices and commercial spaces. The designers brought together their unique perspectives, blending creativity with practicality, and quickly built a reputation for delivering thoughtful, high-quality interiors that leave a lasting impression.

Today, iStudio continues to push boundaries, combining modern aesthetics with timeless elegance. Their mission is simple yet powerful: to create interiors that reflect individuality, enhance lifestyles, and inspire those who experience them. Every project is a testament to their dedication, creativity, and passion for design.
